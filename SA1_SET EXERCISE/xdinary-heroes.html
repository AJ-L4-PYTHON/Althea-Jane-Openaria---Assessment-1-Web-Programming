TROUBLESHOOTING - The 1st Full Album

The album "Troubleshooting" by Xdinary Heroes features 10 tracks showcasing their musical evolution and individuality. Led by the title track "Little Things", the album encompasses a blend of rock and pop genres. All members contributed directly to the production, highlighting their upgraded musical prowess. Collaborations with renowned songwriters like Lee Woo-min "collapsedone" and Hong Ji-sang added depth to the album's sound. Released on April 30, 2024, under JYP Entertainment, the album spans 33 minutes and 8 seconds, offering a captivating journey through diverse musical landscapes crafted by Xdinary Heroes and their collaborators.
